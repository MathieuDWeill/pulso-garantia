# Pitch Deck — 8 Slides

## Slide 1 — Pulso Garantia
Programmable escrow for cross-border service payments in LATAM.

## Slide 2 — Problem
Cross-border service work starts with mistrust:
- Buyers hesitate to pay upfront.
- Sellers hesitate to deliver first.
- Existing platforms are expensive, slow, or not neutral.
- Informal agreements do not scale.

## Slide 3 — Customer
Initial users:
- Freelancers selling to foreign clients
- Small agencies
- Export-oriented service providers
- Buyers hiring across borders

## Slide 4 — Solution
A simple escrow flow:
1. Buyer funds milestone
2. Seller delivers
3. Buyer releases
4. Contract handles refund/dispute path

## Slide 5 — Why Stellar
- Fast, low-cost settlement
- Stablecoin-ready rails
- Soroban smart contracts for escrow logic
- Public testnet/mainnet proof
- Strong fit for LATAM fintech

## Slide 6 — Demo
Show:
- Create escrow
- Fund escrow
- Contract state
- Release/refund/dispute
- Explorer proof

## Slide 7 — Validation
3 interviews:
- Payment trust pain
- Current workaround
- Willingness to use escrow
- Main objection
- Next pilot candidate
