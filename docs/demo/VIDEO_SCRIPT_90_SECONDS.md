# 90-Second Demo Video Script

## 0:00-0:10 — Hook
Cross-border service payments in Latin America still depend on trust. Buyers do not want to pay upfront. Sellers do not want to deliver first.

## 0:10-0:25 — Product
Pulso Garantia is a Stellar escrow for freelancers, agencies, and small exporters. The buyer funds a milestone, the seller delivers, and the smart contract controls release or refund.

## 0:25-0:55 — Demo
Here I create an escrow for a design milestone. We choose buyer, seller, amount, deadline, and terms. The escrow moves from Created to Funded. Once the seller delivers, the buyer releases the payment. The state is contract-backed and every transition can be verified.

## 0:55-1:10 — Stellar
Stellar is the payment rail. Soroban is the trust layer. The contract enforces who can fund, release, dispute, and refund.

## 1:10-1:25 — Validation
We interviewed cross-border sellers and buyers to validate the trust problem, current workarounds, and willingness to use escrow for new commercial relationships.

## 1:25-1:30 — Close
Pulso Garantia turns cross-border trust into programmable settlement.
