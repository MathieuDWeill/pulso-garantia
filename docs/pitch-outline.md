# Pitch outline — Pulso Garantia

## 1. Hook
Millions of LATAM service providers sell work across borders, but payment trust is still handled with screenshots, WhatsApp promises, and manual transfers.

## 2. Problem
- Sellers fear non-payment after delivery.
- Buyers fear paying upfront for unfinished work.
- Cross-border rails are slow, expensive, and opaque.
- Small teams cannot afford legal contracts or marketplace fees.

## 3. Solution
Pulso Garantia is a Stellar/Soroban escrow for service payments:

1. Seller creates a milestone escrow.
2. Buyer funds it with a Stellar asset.
3. Funds are locked until milestone acceptance.
4. Release/refund state is public, auditable, and fast.

## 4. Why Stellar
- Fast settlement and low fees.
- Testnet/mainnet path for real financial rails.
- Soroban enables programmable escrow logic.
- Stellar ecosystem can support anchors, stable assets, and on/off ramp integrations.

## 5. Demo narrative
"Ana in Argentina designs a landing page for Camilo in Colombia. Camilo deposits 850 USDC into Pulso Garantia. Ana delivers. Camilo clicks release. The Stellar transaction becomes the shared proof that payment was completed."

## 6. Market
Start with freelancers, agencies, import/export operators, and B2B service marketplaces in Brazil, Argentina, and Colombia.

## 7. Business model
- Small escrow fee paid on release.
- Premium arbitration/dispute services.
- B2B marketplace API.
- Optional on/off-ramp partner revenue.

## 8. Hackathon proof
- Open-source repo.
- Testnet contract deployment.
- Demo video.
- 3 customer interviews.
- Clear transaction proof.

## 9. Next milestones
- Wallet integration.
- Live testnet contract.
- Stable asset support.
- Dispute resolution flow.
- Pilot with 5–10 freelancers/agencies.
