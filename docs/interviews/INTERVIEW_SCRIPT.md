# Customer Discovery Interview Script

Goal: validate whether cross-border service sellers and buyers actually feel enough payment trust pain to use Stellar-backed escrow.

## Opening
Thanks for taking 15 minutes. I am testing an idea for safer cross-border service payments. This is not a sales call. I want honest feedback.

## Questions
1. What kind of cross-border work or payments do you deal with?
2. How do you currently get paid or pay providers?
3. What happens when you work with a new client/provider for the first time?
4. Have you ever delayed work, asked for upfront payment, or lost a deal because of trust?
5. What tools do you currently use? PayPal, Wise, bank transfer, crypto, platforms?
6. What is painful about those tools?
7. Would an escrow where the buyer funds first but the seller only gets paid after delivery help?
8. Who should decide disputes?
9. Would seeing a public testnet/mainnet transaction increase trust?
10. What would stop you from using this?
11. What would make this a must-have?

## Scoring
Pain intensity: 1-5
Current workaround quality: 1-5
Willingness to try: 1-5
Willingness to pay: 1-5

## Evidence to collect
- Name or anonymized label
- Role
- Country
- Current tool
- Main quote
- Pain score
- Permission to cite anonymized quote
