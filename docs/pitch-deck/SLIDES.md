# Pitch deck copy

## Slide 1 — Title

**Pulso Garantia**

Programmable escrow for LATAM cross-border service payments on Stellar.

## Slide 2 — Problem

Cross-border service work in LATAM starts with a trust gap.

- Buyers do not want to prepay unknown providers.
- Sellers do not want to deliver before money is committed.
- Existing workflows rely on screenshots, manual contracts, slow settlement, or platform lock-in.

## Slide 3 — User insight

From customer discovery:

> “I do not need a bank replacement; I need the client to prove the money is really reserved.”

We heard the same pattern from freelancers, agencies, and merchants: the painful moment is not only payment, it is proof of commitment.

## Slide 4 — Solution

Pulso Garantia lets buyer and seller create a milestone escrow:

1. define amount, seller, milestone, deadline;
2. buyer funds escrow;
3. seller delivers;
4. buyer releases;
5. dispute/refund path is transparent.

## Slide 5 — Why Stellar

Stellar is the settlement and trust layer.

- Soroban owns escrow state transitions.
- Testnet transaction proof is visible.
- Fast, low-cost asset movement fits service-commerce flows.
- The integration is load-bearing: no escrow, no product.

## Slide 6 — Demo

Show:

- escrow creation;
- funding;
- contract status transition;
- transaction proof;
- release/dispute path.

## Slide 7 — Market

Initial wedge: freelancers, agencies, and small merchants in Brazil, Argentina, and Colombia doing cross-border service work.

Expansion:

- B2B micro-invoices;
- milestone payments;
- export services;
- creator/service marketplaces.

## Slide 8 — Business model

MVP fee options:

- 0.5% escrow fee capped per transaction;
- SaaS dashboard for agencies;
- partner revenue with on/off-ramp providers later.

## Slide 9 — Traction / validation

Hackathon evidence:

- 3 discovery interviews;
- repeated trust-gap signal;
- working testnet prototype;
- clear path to on-ramp and arbitration partners.

## Slide 10 — Ask / close

We want to make Stellar the default trust layer for LATAM service commerce.

Pulso Garantia is not another wallet. It is a programmable guarantee that lets people work together sooner.
