# Product spec

## Product name

Pulso Garantia

## Target users

1. Freelancers and small agencies in Brazil, Argentina, and Colombia who sell services across borders.
2. Small merchants and startups who buy services from unknown providers in another LATAM country.
3. Local ecosystem partners who need simple, demonstrable Stellar settlement use cases.

## Core problem

Cross-border service commerce starts with a trust gap. Buyers do not want to prepay unknown sellers. Sellers do not want to deliver before money is committed. Existing alternatives are slow, informal, expensive, or not programmable.

## MVP promise

“Reserve the money, define the milestone, and let both sides see the same settlement rules.”

## Core user stories

### Buyer

- I can create an escrow with seller, amount, asset, milestone, and deadline.
- I can fund the escrow and receive visible proof.
- I can release the funds after delivery.
- I can open a dispute or request refund after the deadline.

### Seller

- I can see that funds are reserved before I start work.
- I can see milestone and deadline in one place.
- I can trust that release/refund follows transparent rules.

### Judge

- I can understand where Stellar is used.
- I can verify that the contract runs on testnet.
- I can see why this matters in LATAM.

## MVP scope

Included:

- Soroban escrow state machine.
- Demo app with seeded scenarios.
- Testnet contract proof.
- Customer discovery evidence.

Excluded for hackathon MVP:

- Full legal dispute resolution.
- Production KYC.
- Fiat on/off-ramp.
- Multi-currency FX quoting.
- Mainnet operations.

## Future roadmap

1. Milestone splitting.
2. Optional arbitrator marketplace.
3. Local payment method/on-ramp partner integrations.
4. Reputation profile based on completed escrows.
5. Invoice export for small businesses.
