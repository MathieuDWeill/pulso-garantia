# 1-2 minute demo video script

## Goal

Make judges understand the product, the Stellar integration, and the real-world pain in under two minutes.

## Script

### 0:00-0:10 — Hook

“Cross-border service work in LATAM often starts with distrust. The buyer does not want to prepay, and the seller does not want to deliver without proof that money is reserved.”

### 0:10-0:25 — Product

“This is Pulso Garantia: a Stellar/Soroban escrow for freelancers, agencies, and small merchants. Buyer and seller define the amount, milestone, and deadline.”

Show the selected escrow card.

### 0:25-0:45 — Stellar funding

“I click fund escrow. This is where Stellar is load-bearing: the escrow state moves from draft to funded and, in the final testnet integration, the buyer’s token transfer is locked by the Soroban contract.”

Click `Fund escrow`. Show status and transaction proof.

### 0:45-1:05 — Release or dispute

“When the milestone is accepted, the buyer releases funds. If there is a problem, either side can open a dispute and an arbiter can resolve it.”

Click `Release funds` or reset and click `Dispute`.

### 1:05-1:25 — Customer discovery

“We interviewed three target users across LATAM service workflows. The repeated pattern was simple: people do not only need payment, they need proof of commitment.”

Show customer discovery section.

### 1:25-1:45 — Close

“Pulso Garantia is not another wallet. It is a trust primitive for LATAM service commerce on Stellar.”

## Recording checklist

- Browser zoom at 100% or 110%.
- Keep cursor movements slow.
- Show README/testnet proof for 5 seconds.
- Keep final video under 2 minutes.
