# Customer discovery plan

## Required by hackathon

At least **3 customer discovery interviews** with accessible evidence links.

## Best interview targets

1. Freelance developer/designer in Argentina selling to foreign clients.
2. Small merchant/startup in Brazil hiring remote providers.
3. Agency or service provider in Colombia working with cross-border clients.

## Screening question

“Have you paid or been paid for a service across borders in the last 12 months?”

Prioritize people who say yes.

## Interview script

1. Tell me about the last time you paid or got paid for cross-border service work.
2. What was the amount, approximate country pair, and payment method?
3. What did you do before the work started?
4. Did anyone pay upfront? Why or why not?
5. What proof did each side have that the other would do their part?
6. What went wrong or felt risky?
7. What did you do when delivery or payment was delayed?
8. Would a neutral escrow have helped? Why?
9. What would make you trust or not trust such a tool?
10. What would you pay for this, if anything?

## Scoring rubric

| Signal | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Strong | They had the problem recently, improvised a workaround, and would try escrow. |
| Medium | They recognize the problem but use a workaround that is acceptable. |
| Weak | They rarely experience the issue or do not care enough to change. |

## Evidence requirements

For each interview, save:

- name or anonymized label;
- persona;
- country;
- date;
- recording/transcript link;
- strongest quote;
- current workflow;
- willingness signal;
- product implication.

Use `docs/interviews/evidence-log.md`.
